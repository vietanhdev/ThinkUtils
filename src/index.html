Battery Care Tips

  • Keep charge between 40-80% for daily use
  • Avoid extreme temperatures (below 0°C or above 45°C)
  • Calibrate battery every 2-3 months (0-100% cycle)
  • Store at 50% charge if not using for extended periods
  • Unplug when fully charged to reduce wear
