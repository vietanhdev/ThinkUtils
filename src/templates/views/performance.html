CPU Governor

How aggressively the CPU raises its clock speed. Powersave keeps it low until work arrives — quieter and longer battery. Performance holds it high, which is faster to respond but runs hotter and drains sooner.

Power Profile

A single switch that tunes CPU, graphics and platform power together. Changing it may move the CPU governor with it.

Turbo Boost

Lets the CPU briefly exceed its base clock under load. Turning it off caps peak speed but noticeably reduces heat and fan noise — useful on your lap or in a quiet room.
